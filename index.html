Inside the sprint

Stay

Protect momentum, preserve current rhythm, accept delayed proximity to home.

Return

Rebuild support network, simplify family logistics, absorb possible career reset.

Redesign

Create a third path: phased move, hybrid geography, role redesign, or school-timing transition.

Why this exists

These decisions get stuck because everything is linked.

One partner's career versus the other's. Children's stability versus proximity to family. Financial upside versus identity, belonging, and long-term regret.

Most families talk around this decision for months without a structure to hold it. Lifecraft makes the real trade-offs visible so you can actually move.
