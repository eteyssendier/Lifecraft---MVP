Apply for the Repatriation Decision Sprint

This pilot is designed for dual-career international professionals and families facing a real decision about staying abroad, returning home, or redesigning life across countries.

What happens next

  1. You submit your application.
  2. You land on a thank-you page immediately.
  3. The submission appears in your Netlify Forms dashboard after deployment.
  4. You review fit manually and invite selected applicants.

What you will review

  • Countries involved
  • Dual-career dynamic
  • Children and schooling context
  • Urgency and level of friction
  • Willingness to join a paid pilot

Which factors matter most? Pick up to 4

Pick the top factors only. Keeping this tight makes the decision pattern easier to review later.

After you deploy on Netlify, form submissions will appear in your Netlify project dashboard.
